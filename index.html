Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What services do you offer?
We offer a range of boat detailing services, including exterior washing, waxing, polishing, interior cleaning, upholstery cleaning, and more.
How long does a boat detailing service take?
The duration of the service depends on the size and condition of the boat. Generally, it can take anywhere from a few hours to a full day.
Do I need to provide any equipment or supplies?
No, our professional detailers come fully equipped with all the necessary tools, equipment, and high-quality cleaning products.
Are your detailers experienced and trained?
Yes, our detailers are highly experienced and trained professionals who have expertise in boat detailing. They ensure top-quality service and customer satisfaction.
How can I schedule a boat detailing service?
You can easily schedule a boat detailing service by filling out our online booking form or by giving us a call. Our team will assist you in selecting a convenient date and time.
